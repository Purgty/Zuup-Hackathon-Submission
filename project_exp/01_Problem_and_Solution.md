# Slide 1: The Problem & The Vision

## The Problem
Public transit networks globally are plagued by **rigid, static scheduling**:
- **"Empty Miles"**: Buses running fixed routes during off-peak hours with zero passengers, wasting fuel and increasing emissions.
- **Stranded Passengers**: Sudden, unpredictable demand surges (e.g., flash rain, event endings) leaving massive crowds waiting at stops while buses on other routes drive by empty.
- **Blind Dispatching**: Human dispatchers lack real-time visibility and predictive tools to dynamically reallocate fleet resources.

## The Solution: ZUUP
**ZUUP** is an AI-powered Demand-Responsive Transit (DRT) platform that turns a rigid bus network into a **fluid, living system**.
- **Dynamic Reallocation**: Automatically shifts buses to where they are needed most in real-time.
- **Proactive rather than Reactive**: Aggregates live QR check-ins and predicts crowd surges before a stop becomes critically overloaded.
- **De-abstracted Dashboard**: Provides municipal operators with a transparent, highly visual command centre to oversee automated fleet movements.
