# Slide 5: Business Model & Value Proposition

## Target Market
1. **B2G (Business-to-Government):** Municipalities and city transport authorities looking to modernize public infrastructure.
2. **B2B (Private Fleet Operators):** Private shuttle networks, corporate transport fleets, and university campus transit.

## The Value Proposition (ROI)
- **Slash Operational Costs:** Drastically reduces "empty miles" driven by buses on dead routes, saving significant fuel and maintenance costs.
- **Automated Dispatching:** Replaces the need for manual, stressed human dispatchers with a scalable, predictive AI engine.
- **Increased Ridership & Revenue:** By guaranteeing buses are actually where the people are, passenger trust in the network skyrockets, converting car-drivers back into public transit riders.
- **Sustainability:** Fewer empty diesel buses idling in traffic means a massive reduction in the city's carbon footprint.

## Future Road Map
- Integration with smart city IoT cameras to measure crowd density without requiring QR scans.
- Passenger-facing mobile app for dynamic ETAs based on reroute calculations.
- Machine Learning models trained on historical city data for week-ahead predictive fleet positioning.
